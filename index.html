Our Mission
“We believe every child deserves to feel celebrated and that no child's birthday goes unnoticed. Our high-school-led nonprofit brings joy and celebration to children experiencing homelessness or chronic illness — and a little light to some of their hardest days.”
What We Do

Small acts, on purpose.

Birthday Celebrations

We plan and deliver birthday celebrations for children facing homelessness or serious illness, so the day still feels like a birthday.

Student-Led

Every celebration is organized by high school volunteers who give their time to make someone else's day brighter.

A Little Light

The goal is never grand gestures — it's showing up, being seen, and making a hard stretch of life feel a little less heavy.
